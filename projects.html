Shape Optimization of a Flywheel

Maximize the moment of inertia of a flywheel under a fixed perimeter constraint. Four parameterizations compared (Fourier, Polynomial, Polar, 3D extension) using L-BFGS-B and Trust-Region solvers. Convergence to the circle confirmed analytically via the isoperimetric inequality. Validated experimentally on a 34g miniature vehicle with 1.8%–5.7% velocity error.
