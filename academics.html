Arts et Métiers ParisTech — ENSAM Lille
Sept 2024 — July 2027
Engineering Degree (MSc level) — Diplôme d'ingénieur

Arts et Métiers ParisTech is one of France's seven leading engineering schools (Grandes Écoles), founded in 1806. Admission is through a national competitive examination taken after two years of intensive preparatory studies — roughly equivalent to gaining admission to a top US engineering school based purely on exam performance.

The Diplôme d'ingénieur is a five-year Bologna-equivalent degree (3 years preparatory + 2 years engineering school), accredited at MSc level by the French national engineering accreditation body (CTI), the equivalent of ABET. Recognized by the French government as equivalent to a Master's degree.

The Lille campus specializes in mechanical engineering, materials science, and industrial optimization — a technical foundation that directly underpins every project in this portfolio.

Lycée Faidherbe
Sept 2022 — July 2024
Intensive two-year preparation in Physics, Mathematics, and Engineering Science for top French engineering schools.

After the French baccalaureate, a small fraction of top students enter CPGE (Classes Préparatoires aux Grandes Écoles) — a nationally selective two-year program equivalent in rigor to the first two years of MIT or Caltech undergraduate coursework. It is designed exclusively for the national competitive entrance exams of France's top engineering schools (Grandes Écoles). Admission is based solely on academic record; fewer than 15% of baccalaureate holders qualify.

The PSI track (Physics & Engineering Sciences) covers advanced mathematics, physics, and industrial sciences at a depth roughly equivalent to a double major in Applied Mathematics and Physics — ~40 hours of structured coursework per week, weekly graded exams and oral evaluations.

Lycée Faidherbe (Lille) consistently ranks among France's top PSI programs, placing students at École Polytechnique, Centrale-Supélec, and Mines-Ponts. These two years shaped the mathematical reflexes that underpin every formulation in this portfolio.
